Who am I?

My name is Samuel Hohenshell and I've got big dreams as a software engineer. I am currently a senior at the University of Minnesota - Twin Cities College of Science and Engineering. I am studying for a B.S. in Computer Science and am on track to graduate Spring 2021. Welcome to my website!

What is the purpose of this site?

This website started as a way to practice web development/Django and evolved into a medium to explain/show off my personal projects, write articles about things that interest me, practice JavaScript by making web apps, expand my HTML/CSS/JS knowledge, and more.

What is on this website?

Allow me to provide a brief, bulletpointed summary of each section of my site...

  • Projects: An overview of all of my personal projects
  • Blog: An unused (but fully functional!) framework for a blog
  • IMC: An overview of my "Influential Media Collection"
  • Articles: A bunch of articles written to share things I think are cool and practice/show off javascript.
  • YT Randomizer: A web app for randomizing large YouTube playlists (since YouTube fails to do so)
